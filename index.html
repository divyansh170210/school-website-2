Campus Life

School Church

Was build in 1978

This church was established by our Rev. Father John. We all go to the church after assembly for 2 minutes meditation. We all pray for our day to be well.



School Library

Was build in 1985

This Library was established by Sr. Rupheena. We all go to library every week in the break time to read some books. Me and my friends love to read books.



School Auditorium

Was build in 1990

This Auditorium was established by Rev. Father Paul. We all celebrate all the functions in the auditorium. Like on Republic Day, Christmas, Independence Day etc. for the acts.
